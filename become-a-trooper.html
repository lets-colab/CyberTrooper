FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS.

Do I need a lot of followers?

No. We care about how you write, not how many followers you have. Some of our best Troopers have under 500 followers.

What do I post about?

Each month you get talking points for 1–2 brands. You choose how to say it in your own words. Never copy-paste — we'll flag it and it won't count.

How do I get paid?

Via bank transfer at end of each month. Rates are revealed after your application is approved.

Can I decline a brand?

Yes. If a brand doesn't fit your vibe, let us know and we'll skip you for that campaign. No penalties.

How long does approval take?

We review every Monday. You'll hear back within 48 hours of your submission being received.
