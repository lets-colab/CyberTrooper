FAQ

YOUR QUESTIONS

When do I get paid?

Payments are processed monthly, 7 days after the billing cycle closes. You'll receive a breakdown of every post and its corresponding payment via WhatsApp and email.

How many posts per week is required?

It depends on your tier. Basic Troopers post 3–5 times per week, Active Troopers 6–10, and Top Troopers 10 or more. You'll agree your tier and cadence during your onboarding call.

Do I need a certain follower count?

No minimum follower count. We care about consistency and engagement quality, not vanity numbers. A small account that posts authentically every day is more valuable to us than a large account that posts once a month.

Can I do this alongside a full-time job?

Yes. Most Troopers post around their schedule — on the commute, during lunch, after work. There are no fixed posting windows, just a weekly cadence you agree to upfront.

What kind of content do I make?

Authentic Threads posts about KL brands — food, beauty, lifestyle, tech, events. The brand gives you a brief with key messages and hashtags. The voice, the story, and the framing are yours. We never ask you to post something you don't believe in.
